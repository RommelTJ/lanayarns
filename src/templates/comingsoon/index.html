What is Lana Yarns?

Lana Yarns is an online yarn store. We will offer brand name yarns as well as our own Lana Yarns product line. Join our email list to know when we launch!

Who are we?

My name is Liza and I love to make things. After starting a knitting podcast and realizing that yarn was taking over my life, I decided to embrace the crazy and go all in. I hope that my humble online store will bring you as much joy as I have had in hand selecting each morsel of fluffy happiness. My other half, Rommel is the talented brains behind the website. His techincal know-how made this virtual store a reality and I cannot thank him enough for his love and unconditional support on this adventure.

Meet Lana!

Named after wool in Spanish (and Italian!) Lana is our virtual pet sheep! She is curious, sweet and full of joy. She will greet you everytime you come to our website and urge you to buy that beautiful yarn with a quick shake of her head. "Baaaah." Lana says hi.
